About Art Heist

Art Heist is a thrilling global competition where teams from around the globe compete in a high-stakes game of strategy, wits, and skill. In this real-world game, players take on the role of red teams, aiming to steal high-value art pieces from renowned museums and galleries. The blue team comprises museum and art security, who work tirelessly to defend the artworks using their expertise and knowledge.

Instead, it challenges participants to rely on their intellect, creativity, and problem-solving abilities to outsmart the defending blue team. The game operates in a capture the flag-esque manner, with each successful art heist earning points for the red teams and impacting the global ranking.

Join this one-of-a-kind global competition and immerse yourself in a world of art, strategy, and excitement. Art Heist is the ultimate test of cunning and teamwork, where only the most ingenious and resourceful teams will rise to the top of the leaderboard.
